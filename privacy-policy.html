Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Effective Date]

This Privacy Policy explains what information Word Explorer ("the App", "we", "us") collects when you play, and how it's used. We built this game to be simple and self-contained — there are no accounts to create and nothing you type or say is ever collected.

1. Information We Collect

Word Explorer does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers.

The only data the App stores is your in-game progress, so that your game picks up where you left off. This includes:

  • Which chapters and levels you've unlocked
  • Your star ratings and completed puzzles
  • Your in-game coin and key balances
  • Daily gift streak information
This progress data is stored locally to your device/app install and is not linked to your identity. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for tracking across other apps or websites.

2. Advertising & Analytics

Word Explorer does not currently display third-party advertising and does not use analytics or tracking SDKs. If this changes in a future update, this policy will be updated first, and — where required — you'll be asked for consent.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party login, social sharing, or data-collection services at this time.

4. Children's Privacy

Word Explorer is a family-friendly word puzzle game suitable for a general audience, including children. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no personal information is collected at all, there is nothing to request the deletion of beyond your local game progress (see Section 6).

5. Data Storage & Security

Your progress is stored using your device's app storage. If you uninstall the App or clear its storage, this data is permanently deleted. We take reasonable measures to keep this storage mechanism secure, though as with any software no method is 100% guaranteed.

6. Your Choices

  • Reset progress: uninstalling the app (or clearing its storage in your device settings) permanently erases all saved progress.
  • Contact us: if you have any question about your data, reach out using the details below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the App evolves (for example, if we add optional ads, cloud sync, or account features). Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
